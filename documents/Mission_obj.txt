Mission Statement:

Organize data on pokemons at a day care facility. Track information indicative of the day care business' performance.

Mission objectives:
Store trainers' information for easy contact.
Keep track of critical data on pokemons.
Log last checkin and checkout time.
Log and retrieve pokemon's activity history and health data at the daycare.
Understand pokemon's performance from such data as reproduction and pokemon evolution.
Get an overview of the business performance of the daycare facility from the number of new friendships created and eggs produced.
